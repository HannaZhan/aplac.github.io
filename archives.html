Thakbong -- 讀墓

A Research and Documentation Projet of
A P L A C
亞太語言文化研究中心
Research Center for Asia-Pacific Languages and Cultures,
College of Humanities and Social Sciences,
National University of Kaohsiung, Taiwan

The Thakbong project is an open-ended project, which in its current stage can best be described as a digital endeavor to document, archive and research epigraphy in the Taiwan Strait and beyond. A short history of the ThakBong project can be found in the description of the Thakbong page.
